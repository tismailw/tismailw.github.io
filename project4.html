Riyadh Cafes Data Analysis

Description:


    Hello Everyone! I took a class at George Mason University, "Scientific Data Mining" and thought i'd share the key points of what I learned/implemeneted in the class.

    My goal for this dataset was to provide a visual correlation of the rating count and see if 24_hours was TRUE or FALSE. After doing so I concluded that there was a correlation between them. Those that are TRUE in this case(green) have a higher rating count than the cafes that are not open 24 hours(red). This is most likely because the stores open for 24 hours will be able to gain more foot traffic yielding more customers and higher count of ratings. The next modeling method I would like to implement in the future would most likely be one of the association rule learning algorithms: FP Growth, Euclat, or Apriori. The reason for doing so would be to find the correlation between rating counts and the location of the cafes as the data set contains two columns regarding latitude and longitude. I would also use Geopandas as it is a useful library in Python.
